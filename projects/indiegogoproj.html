Album Crowdfunding

I'm the keys player for a local Indie Rock band, Suit Up, Soldier. We've played a lot of shows during the summer. At the end of the summer season, we launched an Indiegogo campaign so we could pay for studio time to record an album.

It's been an adventure. I've learned a lot about marketing and social media marketing. There are still a few days left before it ends, but regardless of whether we hit our goal or not, we're able to collect the funds we did raise thanks to Indiegogo's flexible funding option.
